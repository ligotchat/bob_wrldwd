About Me

I navigate the darkness to find beauty in the shadows. My work explores themes of contrast, isolation, and the thin line between comfort and discomfort. Through a dark red and black palette, I create immersive experiences that challenge perceptions and evoke visceral responses.

My artistic philosophy centers around finding meaning in the void, embracing the unknown, and transforming darkness into a canvas for expression.
